All Offline. All Yours.

Built for the World Cup

AllOY26 is not a generic betting tracker. It is a tournament-focused analytics workspace built around World Cup matches, groups, knockout stages, markets, strategies and Betfair CSV history.

Import Betfair CSVs

Import settled bets or betting history from Betfair and validate them against the World Cup calendar.

Analyse every market

Review P/L, ROI, odds ranges, volume, market rankings, scatter plots and per-market drilldowns.

Rebuild your position

See how every back and lay changed your exposure across selections through the timeline of a match.

Track the tournament

Follow groups, matchdays, knockout rounds and a user-built KO tree that creates future matches inside the app.

Compare strategies

Create strategies, assign markets automatically and compare results across P/L, ROI, win rate, drawdown and more.

Own your data

Everything runs locally on your machine. No accounts, no remote database, no subscriptions and no uploaded data.

FAQ

Is AllOY26 free?

Yes. The app is free to download and use.

Does it need an account?

No. There is no login, no account system and no registration.

Does it upload my data?

No. AllOY26 is offline. Your CSVs and data stay on your machine.

Is it only for the World Cup?

Yes. AllOY26 is built specifically around the World Cup structure, calendar, groups and knockout stages.
